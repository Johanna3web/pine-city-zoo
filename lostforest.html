LOST FOREST

Step into the enchanting **Lost Forest**, a hidden world filled with towering trees, exotic plants, and secret animal hideouts. It's a peaceful escape from the busy zoo paths and a paradise for nature lovers and curious explorers.

As you walk along the winding trail, you might spot colorful birds, tree frogs, lizards, and even shy forest mammals. The forest is filled with natural sounds and dappled sunlight — creating the perfect environment to relax and reconnect with nature.

Interactive signs and hidden QR codes along the trail offer fun facts about forest life and conservation efforts. Don’t miss the observation deck for a stunning view over the treetops!

WHERE TO FIND IT?

The Lost Forest entrance is tucked behind the Insect House and leads toward the western edge of the zoo. Look for the wooden archway covered in vines!
