WILD THINGS COFFEE SHOP

Need a break from your wild adventure? Come relax at the **Wild Things Coffee Shop**, the perfect spot to recharge during your visit. Whether you're craving a hot cappuccino, a cold smoothie, or a delicious snack, we've got something for everyone.

Our café offers comfortable seating and a beautiful view of the nearby bird sanctuary. It’s a great place to sit back, watch the wildlife, and enjoy freshly brewed coffee, homemade pastries, sandwiches, and vegetarian options.

Our friendly staff is always ready to serve you with a smile. The cozy atmosphere and nature-inspired décor make Wild Things a favorite hangout for families and solo visitors alike.

WHERE TO FIND IT?

Wild Things Coffee Shop is located just past the reptile house. Look for the wooden patio and jungle vines – you can’t miss it!
