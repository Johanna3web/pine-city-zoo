MONKEY TRAIL

Get ready for some fun and mischief on the **Monkey Trail** – home to some of the zoo’s most playful residents! This lush, elevated trail winds through treetop enclosures where monkeys swing, climb, and show off their acrobatic skills.

The trail gives visitors a chance to observe different species of monkeys in a habitat designed to mimic their natural environment. From the tiny tamarins to the energetic capuchins, there's always something entertaining to see!

Along the path, you'll find educational signs, shaded rest stops, and interactive stations where you can test your own monkey-like reflexes. Keep your eyes open – the monkeys love to surprise guests with their curious nature!

WHERE TO FIND IT?

The Monkey Trail begins just past the giraffe exhibit and loops around the eastern side of the zoo. Don’t forget your camera – this is a guest favorite!
