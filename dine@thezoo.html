DINE@THE ZOO

Welcome to **DINE@THE ZOO** the perfect place to grab a bite, sip something cool, and relax with your family during your wild adventure! Whether you’re craving a hearty meal, a light snack, or a refreshing drink, there’s something here for everyone.

Our food court features a variety of options including burgers, pizzas, salads, ice cream, and vegetarian meals. All food is freshly prepared with kid-friendly portions and seating for both indoor and outdoor dining.

There is a special **KidZone Dining Area** with jungle-themed tables and even a small play space nearby. Don’t forget to stop by the smoothie bar for something fruity and fun!

WHERE TO FIND IT?

DINE@THE ZOO is located in the center of the zoo near the main plaza, right next to the souvenir shop and restrooms. Look for the big canopy and zebra-striped tables!
